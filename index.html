About Me

Hello! I'm Sai Gokhale, a graduate student at Georgia Tech pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science. My research interests lie in developing artificial intelligence systems for complex, real-world scenarios in diverse domains. I aim to bridge the gap between current machine learning models and human-level cognitive abilities. I believe in the power of innovation to drive progress and am always eager to collaborate with others to create impactful solutions.

Projects

Training against Disguises

Mitigating "good-image bias" using synthetic image generation in FER datasets, caused by facial images captured in a lab-controlled environment.

Space Debris Detection

Fusing thermal and visual image properties of space objects to detect and classify the objects into satellite and debris

Demographic Bias Mitigation in FER

Detection and mitigation of historical demographic bias in emotion recognition datasets using multi source domain adaptation

Morphological Analysis for Marathi

A survey of morphological features of Marathi, which have proven useful in applications like speech synthesis, machine translation, information retrieval and spell checking.

Discord Bot for Content Aggregation

Discord bot that scrapes relevant content from the internet whenever the user requests a query.

Air Canvas

An application which tracks the motion of a pen and creates drawings when drawn in the Air. The drawings are saved to clipboard.
